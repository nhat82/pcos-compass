About PCOS Compass

PCOS Compass is dedicated to empowering individuals with Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) by providing a comprehensive platform to track symptoms, manage treatments, find healthcare providers, and connect with a supportive community. The mission is to help you take control of your health and well-being.

Features

  • Symptom Tracking: Log and monitor your symptoms over time to identify patterns and triggers.
  • Treatment Management: Keep track of medications, supplements, and lifestyle changes.
  • Doctor Finder: Locate healthcare providers who specialize in PCOS.
  • Community Support: Connect with others who understand your journey.

Developer

PCOS Compass is founded and made by Nhat Nguyen, a normal woman with PCOS who is passionate advocate for women's health. She is committed to providing a user-friendly and supportive experience for all users.
